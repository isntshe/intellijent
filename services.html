SERVICES

  • • QR Code Check-In

    Skip the line with our convenient QR code check-in system. Scan the code upon arrival to notify pharmacy staff, ensuring a swift and seamless experience.

  • • Copy of Purchase Transaction History

    Patients' transaction history will be at hand, allowing clients to conveniently monitor the specific type and quantity of medicine used in their purchase history.

  • • Availability of Medicine

    When the need is low, our app will show the nearest locations of pharmacies and branches that have the required medicines available.

  • • Notifications

    Doctors and patients will receive different types of notifications. Doctors are notified of the medications required by their patients, while patients receive reminders to buy their meds.
